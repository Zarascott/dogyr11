About Sparky

Sparky Story

A amiable mix of Terriers named Sparky was discovered abandoned on the side of the road. His large, dejected eyes caught everyone's attention; he was skinny and afraid. Sparky is now receiving the tender care he requires at the SPCA. He is looking forward to playing and cuddling in a new home. Sparky doesn't like to be left alone, make loud noises, or move suddenly. He doesn't like water or thunderstorms, but after getting to know other dogs, he becomes friendly.

Things Sparky Likes

Sparky loves playing fetch with her favourite ball, taking long walks in the park, and running around a large garden chasing birds. .

Things sparky Doesn't Like

Sparky is frightened of loud noises, like fireworks and thunderstorms. She also dislikes spending a lot of time alone herself. She dislikes children, therefore if you have children, she will need to grow used to them gradually. Don't force her to like the family too quickly. She is afraid to go on the sidewalk next to the traffic because of past experiences..
