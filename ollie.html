About Ollie

Ollie's Story

Ollie, a loyal dog, lived happily with his elderly owner, an old man. They enjoyed gentle walks and quiet evenings together. One day, the old man passed away peacefully, leaving Ollie alone. Neighbors brought Ollie to a shelter, where he was well cared for but missed his old friend.

Things Ollie Likes

Ollie adores elderly companions and relishes leisurely strolls. Raised on a farm, he developed a deep love for ham, savoring it as a special treat during his days in the open fields. His gentle nature and fondness for slow walks make him a cherished companion wherever he goes .

Things Ollie Doesn't Like

Ollie dislikes being left alone and prefers company. He's not particularly fond of children, preferring calm and quiet environments where he can relax and enjoy the company of adults.
