About dog rescue

The Dog rescue is a vital part of the neighborhood, and without your help, we could not continue to operate.

Joining together, we can provide much-needed assistance to animals and hold those responsible for the mistreatment of these defenseless creatures accountable.

When required, our inspectors pursue legal action to enforce the rules pertaining to the treatment of animals. In our animal hospitals, we provide refuge to animals that are in need and, if it is feasible, we rehome healthy animals.
