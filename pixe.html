Pixes Story

A good-hearted bystander discovered Pixie, a lovely and friendly dog, on the side of the road. They helped to get her to the Dog rescue even though she was afraid and alone. We restored Pixie's health as well as those of her siblings and sisters. Pixie now requires a caring home to take care of her.
